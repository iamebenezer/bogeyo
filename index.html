HOW TO BUY

Create a wallet

Download MetaMask or your preferred wallet for free from the App Store or Google Play. If you’re using a desktop, visit metamask.io to get the Google Chrome extension.

Get some ETH

Make sure you have ETH in your wallet to exchange for $BogeyCTO. If you don’t have any ETH, you can purchase it directly on MetaMask, transfer it from another wallet, or buy it on a different exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Uniswap

Visit app.uniswap.org using Google Chrome or the browser within your MetaMask app. Connect your wallet, then paste the $BogeyCTO token address into Uniswap, select BogeyCTO, and confirm. When MetaMask requests a wallet signature, approve the request.

Switch ETH for $BogeyCTO

Exchange your ETH for $BogeyCTO. There are NO taxes, so you don’t need to worry about setting a specific slippage for your purchase, although slippage might be necessary during periods of market volatility.
